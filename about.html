Mission

Aswan University is committed to providing the best education and research services opportunities to the students and the larger community with a level of quality comparable to the regional and international standards and providing an education based on scientific research to encourage students doing their utmost to become experienced graduates qualified for the changing circumstances.

Vision

To be one of the distinct governmental universities in education, culture, scientific research and knowledge sources. It seeks to estimate its education, scientific research, creative activities, and public serving.
